Manito Park

This interactive visitor map shows the roads, trails, parking lots, and points of interest within J.N. "Ding" Darling National Wildlife Refuge in Sanibel, Florida. If you are in the park, your current location will be marked with a blue arrow.

The refuge is known as "America's Birding Hotspot" and is also home to iconic Florida wildlife, including the Florida manatee, bottlenose dolphin, and American alligator. Use this map to view birding hotspots from eBird, view recent wildlife sightings, and submit your own observations.
